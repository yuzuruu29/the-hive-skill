# Final reference comparison

Review date: 2026-07-15 (Asia/Manila)

## Scope and method

The public Doks.AI spot was used as a clean-room quality reference for pacing, dimensional product presentation, motion density, typography, transition rhythm, and audio energy. No source footage or audio was retained or reused. The reference is a 20-second, narration-free ad; the HIVE master expands that visual grammar to a 66-second evidence-led product story with narration, captions, repository proof, and two social adaptations.

The HIVE scores below are based on the final 1920x1080 master, the 1080x1920 and 1080x1080 adaptations, the final QA stills, and automated media checks.

## Acceptance target result

All required HIVE thresholds are met: opening 8, hierarchy 9, pacing 8, transitions 8, typography 9, product clarity 9, sound 8, climax 9, and overall production 8.

## Validation evidence

- Final compositions: 1920x1080 at 66.048 seconds, 1080x1920 at 42.048 seconds, and 1080x1080 at 30.059 seconds; all are H.264 at 30 fps with AAC stereo audio.
- Master mix: -17.45 LUFS integrated, -2.1 dBFS true peak.
- Sustained-black scan: no segment at or above 0.25 seconds.
- Caption structure: 27 monotonic master cues, 13 vertical cues, and 5 square cues; SRT and VTT validated.
- Safety scan: 53 text files checked with no credential pattern and no retained reference footage.
- Visual QA: final master proof, evidence, climax, end-card, vertical climax, square proof/climax, poster, and thumbnail frames inspected at delivery resolution.

## Known limitation carried into the video

The current HIVE TypeScript check passes. Its latest test run records 258 passed and 2 Windows `EPERM` failures during temporary thread-directory renames, so the video does not claim a current full-suite pass. The limitation is shown in amber and retained in the evidence report.
