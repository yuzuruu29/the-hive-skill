# Sound cue sheet

The HIVE mix is an original, programmatically synthesized electronic bed. It borrows the reference film's functional rhythm—steady technological momentum, state-change accents, and a quiet logo hold—without using or reproducing its music or recordings.

## Music design

- **Tempo feel:** 120 BPM, expressed through 2 Hz bass pulses, 4 Hz harmonic movement, and restrained 8 Hz high-frequency ticks.
- **Palette:** 52 Hz sub pulse, 104 Hz harmonic pulse, filtered pink-noise atmosphere, and short high-frequency confirmation texture.
- **Structure:** sparse hook, fuller orchestration and project-proof middle, strongest low-frequency impact at the swarm climax, then a two-second fade into the final CTA hold.
- **Target mix:** approximately -16 LUFS integrated, stereo, with true peak below -1 dBFS after the final render.
